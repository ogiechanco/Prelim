Rebel

Published on February 21, 2025

This story happened when I was in Grade 8. After the school year ended, my high school friends invited me to go swimming. Excited, I asked my mother for permission, but she said no. Because of my stubbornness, I cried and begged her to let me hang out with my friends. She finally responded with these exact words: "Baka may mangyari sa'yong masama" (something bad might happen to you). Instead of letting me go, she gave me 100 pesos and told me to just go to a computer shop and play.

But I was determined to go swimming. So, I messaged my friends and asked them to help me cover the resort fee and transportation costs. With their help, I managed to escape from the house with my backpack and made my way to the resort.

Since I had snuck out, I didn’t take many pictures that day. But despite that, I had a great time swimming and bonding with my friends. However, I wasn’t careful, and after spending so much time in the sun, my skin noticeably darkened. That’s how I got caught.

When my mom confronted me, the only thing I said was, "Diba? Wala namang nangyari sa'king masama." (See? Nothing bad happened to me.)

And that’s it—hehe.
